Usage: python3 odsx.py [command] [optional argument]

--settings 				 Modify settings related to snapshot, backup, config or script
--servers 				 Modify settings related to space, manager, cdc or Northbound
--cdc 					 Modify settings related to cdc
--northbound 			         Modify settings related to northbound
--security 				 Modify settings related to security
--logs 					 Modify settings related to logs
--objects 				 Modify settings related to objects
--validators 			         Modify settings related to validators
